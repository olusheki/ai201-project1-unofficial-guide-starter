Mundane_Talk_1501: Hi everyone! I’m a rising sophomore and I want to learn more about Grad housing. I tried looking online, but I couldn’t find any pictures of what it looks like. I’ll be in a two-person apartment! I know that Grad comes with a kitchen, but I’m not sure what the amenities are like. Also, I think we have to clean the bathroom and restock things ourselves, but I’m not completely sure. If you’re currently living in Grad and reading this, could you 
share your experience living there? Thanks so much!

- AceAttorneyMaster111: I lived in a grad double sophomore year. The rooms are really big. You each have a separate entrance/exit door, which means you enter from an entirely different exterior door/stairwell. There is a pretty tiny connecting kitchen and a decent bathroom. You have to clean and restock all the things - they’ll start you off with a roll of toilet paper but that’s it.

- Grafton26: My daughter stayed in Charles river during her sophomore year. You have a separate door to enter your room kitchen and bathroom will be in the middle connecting the two rooms. You need to stock all your supplies and you need to clean. My daughter and her friend shared the duties, laundry is in the building. Only thing is it is little farther for classes you have to take the shuttle, if you have morning classes you need to plan accordingly.
